Redistribution Lifecycle

How EcoEats Works

A secure, automated protocol connecting surplus food to local communities in minutes.

01
1. Post Surplus

Donors list surplus inventory (servings, packaging types, and pickup windows). The system immediately verifies it for matching.

02
2. Match & Route

Nearby NGOs receive urgent alerts. They can select items and calculate optimized pickup runs on proximity maps in real-time.

03
3. Safe Handover

Handover is signed off using digital compliance waivers, automatically compiling trace metrics and CO2 offsets in real-time.

Platform Capabilities

Built for Impact & Speed

A secure web infrastructure designed to eliminate food waste barriers.

Optimized Route Coordination

Receivers can claim multiple listings along a single travel path, calculating distance, transit times, and total meal yield dynamically using proximity mapping.

3km Radius Broadcasts

Donors experiencing massive banquet or order cancellations can send instant push notifications to all verified receiver organizations within a 3km radius.

Good Samaritan Waiver

Generate signed digital compliance certificates during handover to verify thermal regulations and food packaging safety, protecting food donors from liability.
