About EcoEats

Building a sustainable future by connecting surplus food with communities in need.

Our Mission

EcoEats is dedicated to eliminating food waste and fighting hunger through innovative technology. We connect restaurants, retailers, and food producers with verified nonprofit organizations, ensuring surplus food reaches those who need it most while reducing environmental impact.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing food waste and its environmental impact, creating a more sustainable food system for future generations.

Community

We believe in the power of community and collaboration to address food insecurity and build stronger, more connected neighborhoods.

Trust & Safety

Food safety and security are our top priorities. We ensure all donations meet strict standards and all partners are verified.

Innovation

We leverage technology to streamline the food redistribution process, making it easier for donors and recipients to connect and collaborate.

Join Our Mission

Whether you're a restaurant with surplus food, a nonprofit serving your community, or someone passionate about reducing waste, EcoEats provides the platform to make a difference. Together, we can create a world where no good food goes to waste.
