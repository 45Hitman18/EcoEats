Donor Liability Protection

Good Samaritan Legal Safety

Under the **Food Safety and Standards (Safe Food and Share Food) Regulations, 2019**, any food donor donating surplus food in good faith is **fully protected from legal liability** for any side-effects resulting from consumption, provided quality handling protocols are followed.

FSSAI Compliance Standards

To guarantee safe donations, donors must comply with baseline handling criteria:

1
Temperature Thresholds Keep cold foods refrigerated below **5°C** and hot prepared meals heated above **60°C** before handover.
2
Grade Food Packaging Surplus items must be packed in clean, sealed, food-grade containers or bags to prevent contamination.
3
Expiry and Traceability Clearly write the item name, preparation date, and estimated shelf life on the packaging label.
