How It Works

EcoEats is a platform that connects food donors with organizations that can use surplus food. Donors post available food, and verified nonprofits can claim and collect it, reducing food waste and supporting communities.
Restaurants, retailers, caterers, farms, schools, and any business or individual with surplus food that meets safety standards can donate through our platform.
Create a free account, verify your information, and start posting food donations. Our team will guide you through the process and ensure compliance with food safety regulations.

Food Safety & Liability

All donations must meet food safety standards. Donors are responsible for ensuring food is safe and properly labeled. Recipients should inspect donations upon receipt.
Good Samaritan food donation laws protect donors from liability when food is donated in good faith. We recommend consulting local regulations and maintaining proper documentation.
We require donor verification and encourage recipients to report any concerns. All users must follow food handling best practices and local health regulations.

Getting Started

Click "Get Started" or "Sign In" and choose whether you're a donor or recipient. Fill out the registration form and verify your information. Setup takes just a few minutes.
Donors need business verification, while recipients (nonprofits) need 501(c)(3) status or equivalent. We'll guide you through the process and approve accounts quickly.
EcoEats is completely free for both donors and recipients. We believe in making food redistribution accessible to support communities and reduce waste.

Troubleshooting

Try logging out and back in, or clear your browser cache. If issues persist, contact our support team with your username and a description of the problem.
Go to your dashboard, find the listing, and use the edit or delete options. If food has been claimed, contact the recipient directly to coordinate changes.
Check your search filters and location settings. If no donations are available, consider expanding your search radius or contacting local donors directly.

About Us

Our mission is to eliminate food waste by connecting surplus food with organizations that can use it, while building sustainable communities and supporting those in need.
We operate as a nonprofit organization funded through grants, donations, and partnerships. The platform itself is free to use for all participants.
